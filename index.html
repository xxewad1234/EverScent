About EverScent

EverScent is a Filipino-inspired luxury fragrance brand celebrating the beauty of the Philippines through scent. Each perfume is carefully crafted to capture nature, culture, and timeless elegance — from the soft bloom of Sampaguita to the cool breeze of Amihan.

We believe fragrance is more than scent — it is memory, emotion, and identity. Every bottle is designed to tell a story that connects you to the soul of the islands.
